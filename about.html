Our History,

At Community4Education, we started as a grassroots movement in 2015, with a small group of community members who were concerned about the state of public education in our area. Over time, our movement has grown to include educators, parents, policymakers, and other community members who share our commitment to improving public education through community involvement.

Our Goals and Objectives

At " Community4Education " our goal is to improve public education by promoting community involvement. Our objectives include:

  • Increasing parent and community engagement in education
  • Supporting educators in their efforts to provide high-quality education to all students
  • Advocating for policies that promote equity, diversity, and inclusion in education
  • Creating partnerships between schools and community organizations to improve education outcomes
 

Our Goals and Objectives

At " Community4Education " , we are committed to:

  • Equity: We believe that every child deserves access to high-quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances.
  • Diversity: We believe that diversity is a strength and that our education system should reflect and celebrate the diversity of our community.
  • Inclusion: We believe that every member of our community has a role to play in improving education, and we work to create inclusive spaces where everyone feels valued and supported.

Our mission is to improve public education by promoting community involvement. We believe that by working together, we can create more equitable opportunities for all children in our community.
